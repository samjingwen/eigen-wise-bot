\begin{minipage}{0.65\linewidth}
Let $F$ be a subfield of the complex numbers. We define $n$ linear functionals on $F^n$ $(n \ge 2)$ by
\[
f_k(x_1,\ldots,x_n)=\sum_{j=1}^n (k-j)x_j,\quad 1 \le k \le n.
\]
What is the dimension of the subspace annihilated by $f_1,\ldots,f_n$?
\end{minipage}

\begin{enumerate}
  \item $1$
  \item $n-1$
  \item $n$
  \item $n-2$
  \item $0$
\end{enumerate}
