\noindent
Let $V$ be the vector space of all real polynomials of degree at most $3$.

\noindent
Define $T : V \to V$ by $T(p)(x) = p(x) + p'(x)$.

\noindent
What is the dimension of $\mathrm{range}\ T$?

\begin{enumerate}
  \item $1$
  \item $2$
  \item $3$
  \item $4$
\end{enumerate}
